Digitizing the Roots of Indian Forestry

Our mission is simple: to build a transparent, efficient, and trustworthy digital foundation for the timber industry in India.

For generations, the timber trade has relied on local networks and middlemen. While these systems have their place, they often lack transparency and can leave landowners unsure if they're getting the true value for their hard-grown assets. We saw an opportunity to bridge this gap with technology.

Axilwood was born from a desire to empower every landowner, from the small farmer with a few mature trees to the manager of a large plantation. By creating a direct line of communication between sellers and verified buyers, we eliminate uncertainty and create a level playing field for everyone.

We are just getting started, and our vision is to grow Axilwood into the most trusted and comprehensive platform for the forestry and agricultural community in India. Thank you for being a part of our journey.
